GA3-240202501-AA1-EV03-FORO

marzo, 2025 Forum Post

In this forum, I discussed environmental sustainability in the workplace and how companies can incentivize eco-friendly practices.

1. How can You be environmentally friendly at work?

- To take care of the environment when we work it is not necessary to carry out great actions, but each one of us can contribute our grain of sand day by day. There are small gestures that help save water and energy resources, as well as the reuse of recyclable elements.

2. What kind of incentives could a green company offer to its employees?

- The implementation of informative talks and inductions on the care of the work environment, constantly to create customs about the good use of our natural resources.

3. In what other ways could businesses involve their employees in environmental issues?

It is no secret to anyone that human beings are sometimes driven to do things out of economic interest. My proposal to attack environmental pollution involves the implementation of recycling machines for objects that will no longer be used. And the company performs the final disposal of these objects in the wholesale or processing place.
